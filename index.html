Hello World!

I’m a former product and sales manager looking to transition my career into the field of UX research and design. My passion for design emerged from over seven years of experience managing the sales of over 400 inventory items (est. cost of $25 million) in the wholesale retail industry. Through this experience, I was given the unique opportunity to learn to think and see the world from the customer's perspectives. My goal is to grow in this exciting field to contribute my knowledge of commerce and use data-driven insights to inform the design of products that are centered around user needs.

I currently live in the Seattle area and I love to hike, bake and play the piano on my downtime.
